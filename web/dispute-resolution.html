Dispute Resolution

Structured process for contested transactions, failed handoff, or mismatch between agreed and delivered conditions.

Step 1: Direct Resolution Window

Buyer and seller should first attempt direct resolution within 72 hours of dispute notice.

Step 2: Evidence Submission

Both parties provide listing ID, offer ID, signed wallet proofs, handoff records, and supporting documents.

Step 3: Escrow Arbitration

If escrow is active, the neutral key holder evaluates evidence and signs release/refund based on policy and evidence quality.

Step 4: External Remedies

Where unresolved, users may pursue mediation, arbitration, or court remedies under applicable law and venue terms.
