Verification

Verification reduces fraud risk by validating listing authenticity, ownership claims, and counterparties before settlement.

Vehicle Verification

Validate VIN consistency across listing, title document, and physical chassis. Check year/make/model alignment and mileage reasonability.

Seller Verification

Require government ID review, ownership proof, and signing challenge on the provided BTC address where possible.

Buyer Verification

For large transactions, perform basic sanctions/AML review per jurisdiction and obtain source-of-funds attestations when required.

Evidence Retention

Keep verification artifacts for audit and dispute handling according to your local legal and compliance requirements.
