How It Works

Simple peer-to-peer marketplace workflow designed for Bitcoin-settled vehicle transactions.

1. Publish a Listing

The seller posts vehicle details, a BTC asking price, and a destination BTC address. Data is validated before listing creation.

2. Receive Offers

Buyers submit offers against a listing ID with their buyer ID and refund BTC address.

3. Complete Verification

Parties verify title, VIN, condition, and identity. Escrow can be required by the seller for risk reduction.

4. Settle and Record

After handoff and settlement, transactions can be marked complete with a BTC transaction reference.

5. Maintain Audit History

Listings, offers, and settlement records provide an operational audit trail for support and disputes.

How are prices set?

Sellers set an asking price in BTC. Buyers can submit bids; the spread is visible and updates in real time.

Can I transact without escrow?

Yes, but sellers can require escrow. For high-value deals, escrow is recommended to reduce counterparty risk.
